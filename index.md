# Welcome

This is the personal website of Sven Franke, showcasing research, projects, and academic work.

## About Me

I am a researcher and data scientist specializing in material handling, warehousing, and intralogistics. My work focuses on automation, mobile robotics, and intelligent warehouse systems.
